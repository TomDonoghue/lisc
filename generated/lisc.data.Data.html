    lisc.data.Data¶

    class lisc.data.Data(term)[source]¶

    An object to hold the word results for a given term.

    label¶

    Label for the current term.

    Type:str
    term¶

    Definition of the search term, with inclusions and exclusion words.

    Type:Term object
    ids¶

    Pubmed article ids for all articles included in object.

    Type:list of int
    n_articles¶

    Number of articles included in object.

    Type:int
    titles¶

    Titles of all articles included in object.

    Type:list of str
    journals¶

    Journals that the articles come from. (Journal Name, ISO abbreviation).

    Type:list of tuple of (str, str)
    authors¶

    Authors of all articles included in object. (Last Name, First Name, Initials, Affiliation)

    Type:list of list of str
    words¶

    Words extracted from each article.

    Type:list of list of unicode
    keywords¶

    List of keywords for each article included in the object.

    Type:list of list of str
    years¶

    Publication year of each article included in object.

    Type:list of int
    dois¶

    DOIs of each article included in object.

    Type:list of str
